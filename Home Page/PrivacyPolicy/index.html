Welcome to Phanthom Global. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you visit or make bookings through our website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal information: name, email, phone number, billing and shipping addresses when you make a booking.
  • Travel details: itinerary, passenger names, passport/citizenship details.
