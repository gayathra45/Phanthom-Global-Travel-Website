Frequently Asked Questions — Phanthom Global

Quick answers for planning, bookings, cancellations, visas, and travel safety. Can't find something? Use the search box below.

Pro tip: Use keywords like destination name (e.g., "Maldives"), booking reference or "cancellation" to quickly filter related answers.

To book: choose a package, select dates and passenger details, then complete payment on the checkout page. To modify an existing booking, sign in and go to My Trips, or contact our support with your booking reference.

We accept major credit/debit cards and many regional online wallets. If you prefer bank transfers or need a pro-forma invoice, contact support and provide your preferred currency and country.

Cancellation rules depend on the supplier and package. Some bookings are non-refundable, others may offer partial refunds depending on timing. Check your booking confirmation for exact terms or reach out to support with your booking reference for an eligibility check.

We provide guidance and offer optional visa facilitation through trusted partners for select destinations. We recommend checking embassy websites for the most current rules—visa policies change frequently.

Baggage rules vary by airline and fare class. Please check the airline section in your e-ticket or contact us with your flight number and we'll confirm the allowance.

Yes — we strongly recommend travel insurance covering medical expenses, trip cancellation, and baggage loss. Some countries require proof of insurance for entry; verify requirements before departure.

We can submit requests for dietary needs and accessibility assistance to hotels and airlines, but approval is subject to supplier policies. Please include requests when booking or contact support at least 72 hours before travel.

Child and group discounts depend on the airline, hotel or tour operator. For group requests (usually 6+ guests) please contact our group desk in advance for a custom quote.

Some packages are priced in multiple currencies. If you need invoicing in a specific currency, contact support before payment and we'll advise whether that's possible for your booking.

Pets are allowed on some flights and in certain hotels. Rules differ widely (vaccination, crates, documentation). Contact us early so we can check options and make necessary arrangements.

Call the emergency support number on your itinerary. We provide 24/7 assistance for urgent issues like medical help, lost passports, or missed connections.
